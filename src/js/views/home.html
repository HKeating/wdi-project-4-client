FckItShipIt™ is a really easy and super-simple task manger for team sprints; less conflicts, more productivty.

We saw there was a demand for a simple sprint manager as traditional ticketing apps did not have an explicit focus on time but rather the tasks at hand leading to tasks being missed or not properly updated.
